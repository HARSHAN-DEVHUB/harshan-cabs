Explore Kerala

Experience the serene backwaters and vibrant culture of Kerala.

Popular Tourist Destinations

Padmanabhaswamy Temple

A renowned Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Vishnu, famous for its intricate architecture.

Fort Kochi

Known for its colonial architecture, art galleries, and Chinese fishing nets.

Alleppey Backwaters

Scenic backwaters ideal for houseboat cruises and exploring local villages.

Munnar

Known for its rolling hills and sprawling tea plantations.

Thekkady

A wildlife sanctuary known for elephants, tigers, and boat rides on Periyar Lake.

Edakkal Caves

Ancient caves with petroglyphs and stunning views of the surrounding hills.

Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary

A haven for migratory birds, perfect for birdwatching enthusiasts.

Varkala Beach

Known for its cliffs and stunning views, ideal for relaxation and water sports.

Kovalam Beach

A popular beach destination with beautiful sandy beaches and water sports.

Bekal Fort

A historic fort overlooking the Arabian Sea, known for its scenic views.

Vembanad Lake

The largest lake in Kerala, famous for houseboat cruises and stunning sunsets.

Idukki Dam

A scenic dam offering beautiful views and opportunities for trekking.

Vadakkunnathan Temple

An ancient temple known for its architecture and festivals.
