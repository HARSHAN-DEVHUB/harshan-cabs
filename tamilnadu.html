Explore Tamil Nadu

Dive into the rich culture and scenic beauty of Tamil Nadu.

Popular Tourist Destinations

Madurai Meenakshi Temple

A historic temple known for its stunning architecture and vibrant festivals.

Ooty

Known for its lush tea gardens and breathtaking hill views.

Kanyakumari

Famous for its sunrise and sunset views over the Indian Ocean.

Mahabalipuram

Home to ancient rock-cut temples and UNESCO World Heritage sites.

Rameswaram

A sacred pilgrimage town with temples and pristine beaches.

Kodaikanal

A peaceful hill station known for its lake and waterfalls.

Thanjavur

Known for the Brihadeeswarar Temple, an architectural marvel.

Yercaud

A charming hill station surrounded by coffee plantations.
