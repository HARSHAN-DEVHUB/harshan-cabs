Explore Karnataka

Discover the cultural heritage and natural beauty of Karnataka.

Popular Tourist Destinations

Bangalore Palace

A stunning palace with Tudor-style architecture and beautiful gardens.

Mysore Palace

One of the most visited monuments in India, known for its grand architecture.

Hampi

A UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its ruins and ancient temples.

Coorg

Famous for its coffee plantations and lush green hills.

Badami Caves

Famous for its rock-cut temples and ancient inscriptions.

Chikmagalur

Known for its coffee plantations and beautiful hills.

Gokarna

A coastal town known for its beautiful beaches and temples.

Chitradurga Fort

An ancient fort with a rich history and stunning architecture.

Sakleshpur

A hill station known for its scenic beauty and coffee plantations.

Nagarhole National Park

A wildlife sanctuary known for its rich biodiversity and safaris.

Jog Falls

One of the tallest waterfalls in India, known for its stunning views.
